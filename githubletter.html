GitHub Love Letter

Dear GitHub,

I love you. And you love everyone else. I couldn’t possibly ask you to be exclusive, because you have so much love to give. A creator, collaborator, and influencer, you are an incredible provider to the coding community. When I need help building a collaborative website, I know who to go to. When I’m stuck on a problem and need advice from fellow coders, you’re always there to show me the way. You read sass like no other server can. Perhaps one day, I can show you what I can do. And how much I really love you.

Sincerely, Elizabeth
